When is the best time to hire an event company or event planner in Kerala?

Hiring an event management company in Kerala is a good way to ensure that your event is perfectly planned and executed. You should hire a planner the moment you decide on the theme and date of the event; however, if you choose the best event management company, it is better to do so at least four to six months in advance to avoid any stress, because at the last moment, most event management companies in Kerala might have busy schedules due to the seasonal peak time.

How much does a destination wedding cost in Kerala?

The cost of a destination wedding in Kerala varies depending on the logistics of the services, the selection of wedding venues in Kerala, the number of guests, the quality of wedding event management companies in Kerala, and the event service quality you expect. However, on average, the cost of a destination wedding, inclusive of wedding decorations, catering services, videography and photography, event entertainment such as wedding dances, live music bands, DJs, logistics, and transportation services from the airport to the event venues, ranges between 14 lakhs and 20 lakhs Indian rupees.
